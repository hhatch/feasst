class Criteria¶

Acceptance criteria for Monte Carlo trials.

Inherits from BaseRandom

Subclassed by CriteriaMetropolis, CriteriaWLTMMC

Constructor.

Parameters
  • beta: inverse temperature: \( \beta = \frac{1}{k_B T} \)
  • activ: activity: \( z = \frac{exp(\beta \mu)}{\Lambda^3} \). Only use this activity with one component fluctuating in number.

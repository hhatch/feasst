Monte Carlo¶

Monte Carlo contains basic trial moves and analysis.

Tutorial¶

A series of files in plugin/monte_carlo/tutorial/ include:

  • analyze.py: utility module to simplify addition of basic analysis to MonteCarlo.
  • test_1_lj_nvt.py: the average energy of a canonical ensemble bulk LJ simulation is compared with published results.
  • test_2_lj_gcmc.py: a short grand canonical ensemble simulation is conducted.

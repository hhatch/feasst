Trial¶

class Trial¶

A trial contains a number of TrialStages. The Acceptance is computed as the stages are enacted, and then sent to Criteria to decide if the trial is accepted or rejected.

Subclassed by feasst::TrialAdd, feasst::TrialAddAVB, feasst::TrialAddMultiple, feasst::TrialAVB2, feasst::TrialAVB4, feasst::TrialFactory, feasst::TrialGrowLinear, feasst::TrialGrowthExpanded, feasst::TrialMove, feasst::TrialRemove, feasst::TrialRemoveAVB, feasst::TrialRemoveMultiple, feasst::TrialRotateCluster, feasst::TrialSwapSites, feasst::TrialTranslateCluster

class Criteria¶

Determine whether to accept or reject a trial. Stores the total energy based on energy changes from each trial. Stores thermodynamic variables relevant to computing the Metropolis probability of acceptance.

Subclassed by feasst::CriteriaFlatHistogram, feasst::CriteriaMayer, feasst::CriteriaMetropolis

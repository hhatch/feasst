void update(const double de)¶

Update total potential energy, peTot_ with energy change, de. Doesn’t update anything else like neighbor lists, individual contributions, etc.

This simple pair updating method doesn’t work with neighborlist. If you’re attempt AVB with ConfigBias, likely you forgot to enable dual-cut. However, this may work with TrialGCA

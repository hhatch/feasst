BaseRandom¶

class BaseRandom¶

This class makes random number generation available to its derived classes by storing a pointer to a random number generator, handing its initialization and restarting, and providing a number of mathematical operations for random number generation.

Inherits from Base

Subclassed by Analyze, Criteria, MC, Pair, Space, Trial

vector<double> eulerRandom()¶

Return random euler angles which yeild uniform sampling on a surface of a sphere (e.g., uniform random orientation).

Euler angles \((\phi, \theta, \psi)\) are defined by rotation \(\phi\) about z-axis, rotation \(\theta\) about new x-xis, then rotation \(\psi\) about new z-axis. The angles exist in the range \(phi [-\pi,\pi], \theta [0, \pi], \psi [-\pi, \pi]\).
